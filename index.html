About Us

Welcome to Sloth Yoga studio, opened specifically for children. Based in Castleknock, Dublin we are never too far away. We are passionate about yoga and benefits it gives to not only adults but children alike. It is our goal to provide fun enviroment for children to explore yoga through playful and engaging classes while building flexibility, strength and mindfulness. Our core belief is that starting yoga practise early builds self-confidence, helps to manage stress and stay focused on daily basis. Our mission is to empower children to lead healthy and balanced lives through the practice of yoga.
